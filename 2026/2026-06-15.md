## 01:49 Community News

### CircuitPython/MicroPython Filesystem: Access via Serial

Access a CircuitPython / MicroPython device's filesystem over the serial REPL. Ideal for boards without native USB (original ESP32, ESP32-C3, ESP32-C6 and others using USB-UART CP2102/CH340/CH9102...). Exposed three ways: FUSE, FTP, or WebDAV. All three sit on the shared library cpfs.py (raw-REPL transport \+ a filesystem API). cpfs.py is also usable directly and as a small CLI \- [GitHub](https://github.com/MakerClassCZ/circuitpython-filesystem).
